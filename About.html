About Page

Operation Brotherhood Montessori Center (OBMC) is an educational institution in the Philippines founded by Dr. Maria Montessori-inspired educator Preciosa Soliven. It follows the Montessori method, which focuses on helping students learn through hands-on activities, independence, and real-life experiences. OBMC aims to develop well-rounded learners by combining academic excellence with character formation, leadership, and social responsibility. The school provides a structured yet supportive environment where students are encouraged to think critically, explore their interests, and become confident, responsible individuals.
