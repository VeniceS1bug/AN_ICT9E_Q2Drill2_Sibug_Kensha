Admissions Page

The OBMC Admissions process is designed to help families understand the Montessori approach and ensure that each student is placed in the environment best suited to their learning needs. The process typically includes submitting basic requirements, completing the application form, and attending an interview or assessment to evaluate the child’s readiness. Parents are also given an orientation to learn about the school’s programs, values, and expectations. OBMC aims to make admissions smooth and welcoming, guiding families as they join a community focused on holistic development, independence, and academic excellence.
