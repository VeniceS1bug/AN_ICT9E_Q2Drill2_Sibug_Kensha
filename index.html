Home Page

O.B. Montessori Center is a progressive Pre-K to 12 school committed to educating future leaders through a blend of tradition and innovation. It highlights the schools Montessori-based approach, which aims to develop self-directed, capable, and responsible learners who can adapt to an ever-changing world. The homepage also presents OBMC’s different academic programs—from Casa to Senior High School—and features its various campuses, giving families an overview of the school’s philosophy, mission, and offerings in one accessible space.
